U.S. Aviation Statistics

This app shows U.S. domestic and international passenger counts by airline and route from the U.S. Department of Trasportation T-100 database.

Use the filters on the left to investigate specific airlines, airports, city markets, or years. The map and charts automatically update when you change filters.

Built by Tom Lee, 2020 using the ArcGIS Javascript API
